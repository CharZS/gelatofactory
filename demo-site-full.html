Irresistibly Delicious Opportunity Award-Winning Desserts

The G Factory concept was created in 2008 by dynamic duo Giuseppe Trivigno and Chris Zammit.

Chris is a pastry chef best known for his accomplishments as a chocolatier and ice-cream specialist. His impressive background includes the study of culinary arts and a decade of involvement in the hospitality industry and well-known hotel chains.

The G Factory has become a local popular spot operating as Creams Factory in home town Tonbridge, Kent, offering an array of artisan desserts, freshly produced on site daily to the highest levels of food hygiene, complemented by a variety of freshly brewed coffee flavours to accompany all kinds of weather.

With Creams Factory having managed the concept successfully over a number of years, The G Factory is now seeking to appoint Franchise Partners on a national level.
