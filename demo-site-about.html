About G Factory

Irresistibly Delicious Opportunity
A Unique Franchise Tale...

Award-winning Desserts Made In Kent

The G Factory concept was created in 2008 by dynamic duo Giuseppe Trivigno and Chris Zammit. Chris is a pastry chef best known for his accomplishments as a chocolatier and ice-cream specialist. His impressive background includes the study of culinary arts and a decade of involvement in the hospitality industry and well-known hotel chains.

The G Factory has become a local popular spot operating as Creams Factory in home town Tonbridge, Kent, Offering an array of artisan desserts, freshly produced on site daily to the highest levels of food hygiene, complemented by a variety of freshly brewed coffee flavours to accompany all kinds of weather.

With Creams Factory having managed the concept successfully over a number of years, The G Factory is now seeking to appoint Franchise Partners on a national level. . The selection of franchisees is seen to be a natural progression for the company’s growth plans. Interested parties need not be qualified chefs or have retail experience in the food industry, one simply needs, first, a strong desire to succeed in putting a smile on the customers’ faces as they tuck into the treats provided and second, to be passionate about the preparation of the various desserts available.

More details on our management and support team can be seen later in this prospectus..

The Ultimate Handcrafted Desserts Business

Being a G Factory Franchise Partner is an ideal opportunity to join up with a business that offers an outstanding variety of artisan products, based on our own original recipes, coupled with first class customer service. Our desserts range from the gelato to cakes, chocolate products and an assortment of pastry delicacies. The G Factory franchise will be in a position to proudly promote all these delightful desserts in its locality.

What makes us different? Many things have contributed towards our success; the quality of our products, the national and international awards which our production team have garnered over the years and the passion we put in into every item on our menu.
Our products come with a quality guarantee of the best ingredients we can source. We do not skimp on quality or time and our main interest is to serve premium quality desserts…on each and every occasion.
Our production team has been in the business for a considerable number of years and their capabilities have been tested over and over again in various national and international competitions, from the Salon Culinaire International de Londres to the Coupe du Monde de la Patisserie, picking up Gold and Best in Class along the way.
We are also different because of our passion for the preparation of hot or cold desserts, whatever the weather and whatever the season. We are not content with producing delicious products, but we are also determined to keep improving and to meet the varied demands of our customers.

We will help you create the perfect venue with our fantastic and unique range of artisan desserts using the chef’s own recipes. You will be able to choose to sell 25-30 different flavours of gelato from a stunning array of 90+ customised flavours, with original recipes and seasonal variations, according to your individual taste, and happily serve fruity sorbets, croissants, crepes, waffles, cakes and other mouth-watering hot desserts. You will enjoy the experience of serving specially roasted coffee with a distinctive blend of coffee beans or delicious milkshakes and smoothies. Let your store be the welcoming place where the whole family enjoys a unique dessert experience.

Takeaway and delivery service is just one more feather in our cap. In Covid times we are still able to bring happiness to our customers in the comfort of their homes depending on the availability of local delivery partners such as Deliveroo, Uber Eats and Just Eat.

We try to play our part in saving the environment so that is why all our containers and packaging are recyclable.

Our greatest motivation is seeing people happy when they enjoy our hot and cold desserts and we would like our franchise to reflect the same. Imagine selling happiness as well as getting rewarded. That is what The G Factory franchise does: sell happiness.

The G Factory franchise is easy to operate and you don’t need to be a chef or have years of experience in the food industry. We have developed a complete package that is already well received. From Day 1 and on an ongoing basis The G Factory will help you to develop your business. The strength and success of our business is a key factor in our growth. Growth and success of the business is essential as we only succeed when you succeed. We grow together.
